Leave your mark on Engineering Walk

Show your support for engineering education. Inspire the next generation of Aggie engineers, honor a graduate or remember a loved one by purchasing an engraved brick on the Engineering Walk at the Zachry Engineering Education Complex. Your gift will support the College of Engineering Excellence Fund. All gifts are tax deductible.

Purchase an 8” x 4” brick for $500.

You can also purchase a commemorative keepsake brick for your home, office or a gift for $150.
