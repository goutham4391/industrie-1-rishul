Maps and Directions

Engineering at Texas A&M

Texas A&M University's engineering buildings are on the far northeast corner of the campus along University Drive.

Visitors may park in:

  • The Northside Parking Garage, at the intersection of Ireland Street and University Drive,
  • The limited visitor parking in Lot 51 (pay-by-space parking).

For more parking information, visit the Transportation Services visitor parking page.
